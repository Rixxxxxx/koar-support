Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026

KOAR ("we", "our", "the app") is a strength training application for iOS and Apple Watch. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Workout and training data. Exercises performed, sets, reps, weights, RPE, rest times, workout duration, and program configuration. This data is stored locally on your device and synced across your devices via Apple iCloud. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Wellness check-in data. Self-reported energy, sleep quality, and stress levels entered before each workout. This data is stored locally and via iCloud only.

Apple HealthKit data. With your explicit permission, KOAR reads and writes the following HealthKit data types:

  • Read: Heart rate, resting heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), sleep analysis, active energy burned, and workout history.
  • Write: Workout sessions (strength training type) including duration and active calories.

HealthKit data is used solely to calibrate workout intensity, display live heart rate during exercise, and log completed workouts to your health record. HealthKit data is never transferred off your device, never sent to our servers, never sold, and never used for advertising. This is enforced by Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

AI workout generation. When you use the AI Workout Generator feature, the text prompt you enter and your profile settings (fitness level, goal, equipment, training days, session duration) are sent to our server to generate a workout plan. We do not store your prompts or generated plans on our server. The request is processed and immediately discarded.

Subscription and purchase data. Subscription status is managed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information, credit card numbers, or Apple ID credentials.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or account credentials. KOAR does not require an account.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or analytics data.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not collect location data.

Data Storage and Sync

All workout data, settings, and progression history are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. If you enable iCloud, this data syncs across your Apple devices using Apple CloudKit. Apple encrypts this data in transit and at rest. We cannot access your iCloud data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. The only network request KOAR makes is to our AI workout generation server when you explicitly use that feature, and no personal data is included in that request beyond your fitness profile settings and the prompt you type.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account, you have full control over it. Deleting the app removes all local data. To remove iCloud data, you can do so through your device's iCloud storage settings.

Children's Privacy

KOAR is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at koarsupport@gmail.com.
